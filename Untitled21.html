CONTENT TYPE 1

Entertainment Content Type

Vlogs with a hint of Music may be fit for promoting the concert; while helping spread awareness about this concert’s causes, they also add a touch of entertainment through the aspect of music. This content type follows current trends, which will help increase the viewership of the promotion. These trends include; Behind-the-scenes preparations for the artist, insights regarding the artist, and the topic of live music.

Data was actually collected in “Journal of Retailing and Consumer Services“ that shows the huge impact of vlogs and the rise of it’s popularity in recent years.

Basing from our own findings this time from watching the given content types, we found that vlogs tend to be more casual, trendy, and engaging. This content type will benefit the promotion of the concert by allowing the artist to connect with an audience and provide them with further insights and rehearsal elements. This content type will make the audience feel more invested in the concert and in attending. This content type will additionally address people’s needs to be entertained, and relate.

CONTENT TYPE 2

Educational Content Type

The other possible content type to choose from is awareness and educational content. With the topic of Sitio Bebe and rural areas being the focus of the concert, content types that focus on spreading awareness may be compatible with the promotion of the concert. Additionally, it may help the concert gain more sales as people learn more about the upbringing of attending it.

An article from Goodspero actually shows the influences and importances of producing it. It can also be utilized as a marketing tool. Upon watching more videos from this content type, the organizers found that this content type tends to focus on the educational aspects, providing the audience with important information about the causes of the concert. It highlights the call to action and encourages the audience to attend by spreading awareness of the possible impacts this concert may have. The emotional engagement that this content type may provide viewers may truly help in the promotion of the concert.

This content type will address people’s needs to be educated and help us spread awareness for better causes.

CHOSEN CONTENT TYPE:

Entertainment Content Type

Our Chosen solution to promoting our website is all about entertainment. We will request our artist to film vlogs about our concert and show previews of the show, especially during practice and rehearsals.

We chose entertainment content because it’s a great way to connect with a wide audience and keep them interested. Research shows that people prefer fun and engaging content like vlogs and music, which are more likely to be shared and attract attention.

By showing behind-the-scenes moments and artist insights, we can create excitement for the concert while making the audience feel more connected to the artist. Entertainment content also feels more natural and enjoyable, which helps build trust and keeps viewers watching. This will increase awareness and make people more likely to attend the concert.
